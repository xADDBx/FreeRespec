[h1]FreeRespec[/h1]
Allows changing the Respec Cost Behaviour.

[h2]Usage[/h2]
To use:
[olist]
[*]Click the button in the FreeRespec mod section of the UnityModManager GUI.
[*]Pick one of the [b]four different modes[/b]:
[list]
[*][b]Free[/b]: Respecs are always free.
[*][b]Always pay x[/b]: Respecs will cost a certain amount you can choose.
[*][b]Pay at most x[/b]: Respecs will cost the normal amount but stop getting more expensive after reaching a certain amount you can choose.
[*][b]Normal Game Behaviour[/b]: Literally normal game behaviour.
[/list]
[/olist]


[h2]Installation[/h2]
Installation is similar to [url=https://github.com/cabarius/ToyBox/wiki/Installing-Toybox-for-RT-(Steam-Workshop-Edition)] how it's done with ToyBox[/url].
